Afghanistan Earthquake Relief Fund


A powerful earthquake in Afghanistan killed more than 1,000 people and wounded and displaced hundreds more on June 22, 2022. Your donation to the Afghanistan Earthquake Relief Fund will provide emergency assistance to people in need and support long-term recovery efforts led by local organizations.



East Africa somalia Hunger Relief Fund


Across East Africa, millions of people are facing famine. A severe climate-crisis-induced drought is causing the dire situation, coupled with regional conflict, the prolonged impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, and soaring food prices tied to the war in Ukraine.



India COVID-19 Emergency Relief Fund


This project will ensure oxygen support to 152 critically ill COVID-19 patients, food and ration support to 500 vulnerable families, and home-based care and treatment to hundred COVID-19 patients who have no financial and emotional support during the crisis. Beneficiaries will be supported across India.
